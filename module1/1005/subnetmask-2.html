Subnet mask

You now know that the network ID is 192.168.123.0 and the host address is 0.0.0.132 because you have a subnet mask of 255.255.255.0. When a packet with the destination address 192.168.123.132 arrives on the 192.168.123.0 subnet (from the local subnet or a remote network), it will be received and processed by your computer from the network.
