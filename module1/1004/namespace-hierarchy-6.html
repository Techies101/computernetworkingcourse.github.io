Namespace Hierarchy
Top Level Domain .MIL

The space name mil is the top level domain in the DNS of the Web for the US Branch of Protection and its auxiliary or partnered associations. The name is gotten from the military. 

Top Level Domain .MUSEUM

Museum is a top-level domain in the DNS of the Web utilized only by exhibition halls. The main purpose of this domain is to hold a section of the DNS name space held for the utilization of exhibition halls; a name space whose shows are characterized by the museum center's local area.
