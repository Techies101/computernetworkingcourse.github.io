Wide Area Network

A network that reaches out over an enormous geological zone like states or nations.

A Wide Area Network is very greater network than the LAN.

Some of examples of Wide Area Networks :

  • Mobile Broadband
  • Last Mile
  • Internet

Mobile Broadband: A 4G network is generally utilized across a locale or country.

Last mile is a telecom company that used to provide the internet services to the customers in hundreds of cities by connecting their home with fiber.

Internet also one of the largest Wide Area Network.
