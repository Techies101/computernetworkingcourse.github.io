Network Layers of OSI Model

Transport Layer (layer 4)

Responsible for the given link through flow control, segmentation/desegmentation, and error control. Some protocols are state- and connection-oriented.

This suggests that the transport layer will keep a track of the segments and conduct people who fail delivery. Also provides successful data transmission and sends the following information if no errors occurred.

The transport layer creates segments out of the message received from the applying a layer. Segmentation is the method of dividing the extended messages into pieces of information.
