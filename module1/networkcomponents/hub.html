Hub

At the point when PC demands for some data from an organization, it initially sends the solicitation to the Hub through link. Center point will communicate this solicitation to the whole organization. Every one of the gadgets will check if the solicitation has a place with them. If not, the solicitation will be dropped. Nowadays, the use of hub is obsolete, and it is more advanced computer network components such as Switches and Routers.
