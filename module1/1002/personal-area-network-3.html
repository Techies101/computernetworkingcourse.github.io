Personal Area Network (PAN)

Some of examples of Personal Area Network :
  • Body Area Network
  • Offline Network or Home Network
  • Small Home Office

Body Area Network: a network that uproot with a person. For example, a mobile network moves with an individual. Assume an individual builds up network connection and afterward makes a connection with another gadget to share the data.

Offline Network or Home Network can be generated inside our home. Offline network was created to manipulate other devices such as computers, printers and other televisions which are not connected to the internet.

Small Home Office is used to utilize a variety of devices to the internet and to a corporate network using a VPN.
