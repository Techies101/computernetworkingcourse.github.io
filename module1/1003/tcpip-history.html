TCP/IP Model

History of TCP/IP Model

1974: publishing the A protocol for Packet Network Interconnection paper that describes the TCP/IP Model written by Vin Cerf and Bob Kahn.
1978: called TCP/IP because of new protocols by testing and the development of the language.
1982: during this time ARPANET decide to change the standard language from NCP to TCP/IP.
January 1, 1983: ARPANET changed to TCP/IP.
1990: TCP/IP was evolved to fill the changing requirements of the Internet. Totally finished the existence of ARPANET in this era.
